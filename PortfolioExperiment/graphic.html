GLAD #forAll

✖

GLAD is a non-profit committed to fighting legal battles for LGBT+ people in New England. This flier about LGBT rights was designed for print distribution at high schools in Massachusetts. It is filled with details about rights at school for LGBT+ students, so that they may be better served by the administration.

This is one graphic of many created to support GLAD's lawsuit against the Trump administration over the transgender military ban.

If It's Your Butt: Grab It

"If It's Your Butt, Grab It: The Beach is Not Your Ashtray" was a social campaign designed to reduce cigarette butt waste along coastlines. This project included designing a full range of digital and print media, including marketing materials, billboards, a website, mailers, and even creating a full-scale model of a receptacle.
