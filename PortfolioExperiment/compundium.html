The Compundium is a webcomic about competitive Super Smash Bros- a niche comic for a niche community within the larger gaming world. The strips and gags consist almost entirely of references to community figures, lore, memes, and other pieces of Smash mythos.

Most of the characters featured in these comics are caricatures of real-life people - usually professional ‘Smashers,’ as community members are called. The strip began in August 2017, and can be found on theCompundium.com.

This comic was an amazing opportunity to engage with a community through art.
